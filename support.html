NoSkip — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nahom Ghirmay ("we", "us", "NoSkip") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the NoSkip mobile application (the "App"). We are the data controller for the purposes of UK data protection law (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018) and, where applicable, the EU GDPR.

If you have any questions, contact us at nahomghirmayeyob@gmail.com.

1. What NoSkip is

NoSkip is a voluntary accountability app that helps you keep a gym schedule you set for yourself. You choose your training days, set an optional personal stake, invite a person you trust to act as your accountability partner, and confirm your sessions using your location and an optional photo. NoSkip does not collect, hold, or transfer any stake amount — any stake is a figure tracked in the app and settled privately between you and your partner outside the App.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide the core accountability features (scheduling, verification, partner review, the stake ledger).
  • To send reminders and notifications you have enabled.
  • To manage your subscription entitlement.
  • To keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and respond to content reports.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

Legal bases (UK/EU GDPR)

We rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the App you signed up for), consent (for location, camera/photos, and push notifications, which you can withdraw at any time in your device settings), legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, and improving the service), and legal obligation where required.

4. Sharing your data

We share data only as needed to run the App:

  • Your accountability partner sees the session photos and confirmation information you submit to them, and your outstanding stake balance. Choose a partner you trust.
  • Apple processes subscription payments. We never receive your full payment details.
  • Superwall manages the subscription paywall and entitlement.
  • Google Maps / Places powers the map and location features.
  • Our hosting and notification providers store data and deliver push notifications on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

5. International transfers

Some of our providers (including Apple and Superwall) are based in the United States, so your data may be transferred outside the UK/EU. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

6. Data retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active. Some records (such as activity logs and the stake ledger) are kept for the operation and integrity of the service. When you delete your account, we remove or anonymise your personal data as described below.

7. Deleting your account and data

You can delete your account at any time from within the App (Settings → Delete account). Deleting your account cancels your active commitment, stops all reminders, revokes your partner links, and does not create any stake or penalty. We then delete or anonymise your personal data — including your verification photos, precise location data, name and email, and your identity in activity records. Deleting your account does not, by itself, cancel an Apple subscription; manage that in your Apple ID settings.

8. Your rights

Under UK/EU GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to our processing of your data, and to data portability. To exercise any of these, email nahomghirmayeyob@gmail.com. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk, or your local EU supervisory authority.

9. Children

NoSkip is not directed to children. You must be at least 18 years old to use the App. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age; if you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard the information you entrust to us.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where changes are significant, notify you in the App.
